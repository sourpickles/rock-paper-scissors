Welcome to Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Before you start the game, lets go over some simple rules:

  • You will be playing against a your computer. That means good luck trying to predict its choice.
  • There are three buttons at the bottom of the screen. These are youre options listed in order of rock, paper, and scissors.
  • The score will be tracked on screen. Whoever reaches 3 wins first wins the game.
  • If after your first game you would like to replay, please hit the refresh button on your computer.

Thats all! Click the X to play the game, good luck!
